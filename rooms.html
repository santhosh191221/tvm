Single Room

Perfect for the solo traveler, our Single Room offers a comfortable twin bed, a dedicated work desk with high-speed Wi-Fi, and an en-suite bathroom. Enjoy peace and quiet with all the essential amenities for a productive or restful stay.

Double Room

Ideal for couples or friends, the Double Room is spacious and comfortable, featuring a queen-sized bed and a stunning city view. It includes a cozy seating area, a flat-screen TV, and complimentary toiletries to make your stay enjoyable.

Suite

Experience ultimate luxury in our Suite. This expansive room includes a separate living area, a king-sized bed, and a private balcony. The lavish bathroom features a jacuzzi tub, and guests enjoy exclusive perks like complimentary breakfast and evening cocktails.

The Penthouse

Our exclusive Penthouse offers unparalleled views of the city and Arunachala hill. Featuring two bedrooms, a private terrace, and a fully equipped kitchenette, it's the pinnacle of luxury for discerning guests.

Studio Apartment

For those planning a longer stay, our Studio Apartment provides all the comforts of home. It includes a small kitchen, living space, and a comfortable sleeping area, perfect for extended visits.
